Introducing, Fruitly.

A clever way to save the world

Use Fruitly, save the world and money!

Fruitly is making it cheaper and easier to order baskets of fruit that the grocery store otherwise would throw away. Our customers are either private consumers or businesses that need extra energy and healthy food for a low price, whilst also contributing to a more sustainable planet.

In addition we hope to reduce waste by including information and recipes that utilizes fruit that you might not feel too inclined to eat. In our app you will see a recipe section that includes recipes that we recommend. Our latest feature is that you can share your own recipes and contribute to solutions to reduce fruit waste. Every month for a year we will give out a Fruitly gift-card to the most upvoted recipe of the month.

In our app you can order packages of different sizes containing different fruit. This way we can ensure that you pick a size that fits your needs. We have packages from mini-pack all the way to our special 10-pack. (varies by local grocery store capacity)

How to get involved.

Making a difference is easier now than ever! All you have to do to get started is to head over to where you usually get your apps and download Fruitly! Within minutes you can begin to explore what amazing deals your local grocery stores have available for you.

Thanks for helping us towards a better future!
